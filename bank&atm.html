Bnak & ATM

The bank office at Varna Airport is located in the immediate vicinity of the check-in area, just before the security checkpoint in Terminal 2.

Opening hours:
Monday - Friday 9.00 - 14.00 / 15.00 - 18.00
Saturday: 10.00 - 18.00
Sunday: 10.00 - 17.00

You can find an ATM just before the security checkpoint. The machine accepts most credit cards.
