Check-in

All about how to prepare for check-in

Before you arrive at the airport, make sure you have packed your luggage, including carry-on luggage, according to security requirements Security and baggage check . When you are at the airport, check in at the “Departing passengers” area. To save time, have your ticket and passport ready, and visa if necessary. After checking in your luggage and receiving your boarding pass from the baggage check-in counter, head to the security checkpoint.

How early should you be at the airport?

Follow the check-in instructions that you received with your reservation. We recommend that passengers check in two hours before their flight. Even if you have checked in online, it is a good idea to allow enough time to go through security and be at the gate to be taken to the plane.
We recommend that you check in early enough to have time to enjoy your favorite food and drinks, as well as shopping opportunities.
