To and from airport

By car

Varna Airport is located 8 km west of the city of Varna. You can reach the airport by car in less than 20 minutes.

GPS information: Latitude and longitude: 43.2368° N, 27.8271° E

Address: Varna Airport Varna 9000 Bulgaria

By bus

To get to and from Varna Airport by public transport, use bus number 409. In less than 20 minutes you can reach the city center and in about 50 minutes you can reach the Golden Sands resort complex.

Bus number 409 runs every 15 minutes and stops in front of Terminal 2, Arrivals. Tickets can be purchased inside the bus. Real-time timetable of public transport in Varna.

By taxi

Taxi services at Varna Airport are available 24 hours a day. You can find the yellow Triumph Taxi cars in front of Terminal 2.
