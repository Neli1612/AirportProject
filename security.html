Security

Safety and security are top priorities for all passengers. All departing passengers must go through a security check. All checked baggage is subject to mandatory screening by security officers at Varna Airport.

How to prepare your hand luggage?

Only limited quantities of liquids can be carried in hand luggage. The following restrictions apply to all liquids, creams, gels, pastes and aerosols that go through security screening.

  • Liquids must not exceed 100 ml
  • Must be in individual packages, measuring 20 cm x 20 cm:
    • Use a transparent and self-sealing bag
    • Make sure the bag can be closed with all liquids inside
  • At the security checkpoint, you must remove the transparent bag with the liquids from your hand luggage and place it in the x-ray container
  • Liquids exceeding 100 ml. will not be allowed for screening - please pack them in your checked baggage that goes into the aircraft hold

The following exceptions to the 100ml rule are possible.

  • Medicines
    • You will need prior approval from the airline and the airport you are flying to
    • Medicines must be accompanied by a document, for example, a prescription or a document from your doctor
  • Baby food and milk
      You can carry baby food, baby milk and sterilised water in your hand luggage. This includes:
    • soy milk for babies
    • sterilised baby water (must be in a baby bottle)
    • baby milk
    • baby food of different consistencies
    You can carry the amount you need for your trip, in some cases this may mean more than 100ml.
