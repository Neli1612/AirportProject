Travel with kids

If you are traveling with small children, Varna Airport offers facilities that will help you and make your trip easier. The room for mothers with children is located in the "Departing passengers" area after security check. The place is calm and comfortable and you can take care of your child before boarding the plane. Travelers to destinations outside the Schengen area receive additional service from an employee for using the room for mothers with children.
Diaper changing facilities are located in the toilets in Terminal 2.
