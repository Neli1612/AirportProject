Medical services

Our medical team is here to help you 24/7

If you need medical assistance while at Varna Airport, contact our Medical Center. Our professional team of doctors and nurses are available 24 hours a day to assist you. The medical assistance office is located next to the Flight Information counter in Terminal 2.
