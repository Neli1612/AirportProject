Lost & Found

We are here 24 hours a day to help you!

If you have lost something at Varna Airport or your luggage has not arrived with your flight, we will help you find what you are looking for again. The Baggage Claims office will take care of tracking your luggage. If you would like to check the status of your lost luggage, please fill in the fields in the feedback form. Our employees will contact you by e-mail.
If you have forgotten something on board the plane, please contact your airline directly.
