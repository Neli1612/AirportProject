Accessible Travel

Varna Airport is accessible to people with reduced mobility. Please contact and inform your airline, travel agent or tour operator with details of your assistance requirements before your flight.
We recommend that you arrive at the airport no later than two hours before your flight. Once at the airport, you can contact us using the helplines located in the long-term and short-term parking areas.

    Facilities at Varna Airport for passengers with reduced mobility include:
  • TLong-term and short-term parking areas for passengers with reduced mobility
  • Specially adapted facilities, including toilets, all clearly marked
  • Special means of transportation from the terminal to the aircraft
  • Well-trained staff to meet the special needs of people with reduced mobility
