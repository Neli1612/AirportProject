VIP

WELCOME TO THE VIP LOUNGE AT VARNA AIRPORT

Passengers looking for exclusive and priority services at Varna Airport are cordially invited to enjoy a comfortable and relaxed stay by choosing our VIP services.

The VIP service begins with the passenger's arrival at the terminal. There, an employee of our VIP team welcomes the guest and is responsible for handling all formalities such as: passenger and baggage registration, fast security check and check-in in our elegant VIP lounge with a beautiful terrace and a view of the platform. Guests of the lounge traveling to a destination outside the Schengen area must anticipate that the border control for their flight is after their stay in the lounge.

The VIP lounge offers an assortment of selected specialties and drinks, newspapers, magazines, television and free internet. The exciting experience ends with a separate transfer to the aircraft.
