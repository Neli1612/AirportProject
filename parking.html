Parking

Whether you are meeting family or guests, or simply traveling to a destination in the world, if you need a convenient parking space for your car, use the parking lot in front of Terminal 2 for hourly, daily or long-term stays.
The free stay for meeting or sending off guests is 10 minutes. A parking service fee applies to all vehicles whose stay on the territory of Varna Airport is more than 10 minutes from the moment of entry.
