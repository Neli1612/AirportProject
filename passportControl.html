Passport Control

Make sure you have all your documents ready for your trip

Passengers travelling to and from the Schengen area do not undergo border checks, but must carry a valid travel document.
Passengers travelling to and from outside the Schengen area undergo border checks. For greater convenience, arriving passengers and e-passport holders can use automated counters instead of queuing up to see a Border Police officer. The electronic counters use facial recognition technology and fingerprints to verify the passenger's identity.

Traveling with children or minors abroad

In connection with the increasing cases of minors and minor Bulgarian citizens being denied entry to an international flight, as well as in order to better inform travelers, please visit the following link to information published by the General Directorate of Border Police for travelers abroad
